# QA Scorecard (Simulation)

This scorecard simulates internal QA checks used to ensure rating consistency.

## Consistency Checks

- Intent correctly identified? ✅
- Rating scale applied consistently? ✅

## Common Rater Pitfalls (Avoided)

- Overrating forums for YMYL

- Not penalizing pages with heavy spam signals
