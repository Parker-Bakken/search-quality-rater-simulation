# Search Result Rating Scale

Results are rated on a 0–4 scale based on usefulness and intent satisfaction.

## 4 — Fully Meets
Perfect match for user intent.
Provides the exact information or solution needed with minimal effort.

Example:
Official documentation solving the user’s problem directly.

---

## 3 — Highly Meets
Strong match for user intent.
Provides very helpful information but may require small additional steps.

Example:
High-quality tutorial or trusted article covering the topic thoroughly.

---

## 2 — Moderately Meets
Relevant but incomplete.
Helps partially but does not fully satisfy the user need.

Example:
Article touching the topic but lacking depth.

---

## 1 — Slightly Meets
Weak relevance or limited usefulness.

Example:
Page mentions the topic but does not directly answer the query.

---

## 0 — Fails to Meet
Does not address the query or is spam/malicious.

Examples:
Broken pages  
Scam sites  
Irrelevant content
